# CLITZ.xyz

CLITZ.xyz is a women-centered safety, health, privacy, memory, mutual aid, review, prayer, debate, and creator-scaffold platform.

It is being built for women who need care, witness, verification, privacy, and practical support without being forced into unsafe exposure.

![CLITZ hero](../assets/hero/hero-image.png)

## A Galaxy Of Female

CLITZ brings together privacy-safe maps, moderated directories, missing women and girls visibility, provider records, domestic violence and assault resources, female-review systems, mutual aid, fundraisers, prayer rooms, debate topics, and protected creator profile scaffolding.

The first public implementation is CLITZ Galaxy Core, a private WordPress MVP designed around moderation, source quality, safe location rules, and audit trails.

## What The MVP Supports

- Missing women and girls visibility
- Provider and service directories
- Domestic violence and assault resource listings
- Reviews with safety acknowledgments
- Mutual aid and fundraiser records
- Prayer and debate scaffolding
- Verification badges and source logs
- Abuse and update reports
- Legal placeholder pages for attorney review

## Privacy Comes First

Sensitive records default to hidden or approximate location. Exact public markers require explicit admin safety consent. Private contacts, admin notes, safe-house addresses, and non-consented survivor locations are not public-facing.
