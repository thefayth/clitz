# Brand Style Notes

CLITZ should feel direct, protective, woman-centered, editorial, alive, and serious without becoming sterile.

## Voice

- clear
- warm
- women-centered
- privacy-aware
- receipt-driven
- practical
- not corporate-flat

## Visual Direction

The public asset set uses the approved editorial CLITZ hero as the lead visual. It carries the strongest first-read signal for the project: women-centered, modern, protected, public, and bright enough to avoid fear-first safety branding.

## Public-Safe Asset Rules

- Use approved editorial visuals.
- Do not publish raw admin screenshots.
- Do not publish moderation queues.
- Do not expose prompts or private workflows.
- Crop or abstract any operational screenshots before public use.
- Keep sensitive records out of image alt text, filenames, captions, and metadata.
