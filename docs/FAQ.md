# FAQ

## Is this open source?

No. This is a protected public project surface. It is not a source release.

## Can I reuse the CLITZ code, brand, images, or docs?

No reuse is granted by this repository. CLITZ is All Rights Reserved unless Faith Cheltenham / XXYYZZ Society LLC gives written permission.

## Why WordPress first?

WordPress gives the project a fast public layer for pages, maps, directories, forms, moderation, and legal placeholders while the private app and operating tools remain protected.

## Why are exact locations restricted?

Some records involve missing women, survivors, providers, shelters, and sensitive care contexts. Location privacy is a safety feature, not a missing feature.

## Are legal pages final?

No. Legal, medical, crisis, fundraiser, adult content, and data removal pages are placeholders for attorney review.

## Is private creator hosting live?

No. Creator profile reuse and legal hooks are scaffolded only. Adult/private uploads, subscriptions, and processor flows are excluded from the first wave.
