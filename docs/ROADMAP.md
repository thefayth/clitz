# Roadmap

## Phase 1 - First-Wave WordPress MVP

- Privacy-safe maps and directories
- Pending submissions
- Moderation dashboard
- Verification badges
- Abuse and update reports
- CSV dry-run imports and exports
- Legal placeholder pages
- Mutual aid, prayer, and debate scaffolding

## Phase 2 - Launch Hardening

- WordPress draft deployment
- Backup and rollback receipts
- Screenshot receipts
- Mobile and accessibility pass
- Spam and rate-limit tuning
- Admin 2FA verification
- Operational review cadence

## Phase 3 - Community Expansion

- Richer member profiles
- Local chapters or circles
- Safer notifications
- Moderator training materials
- Dispute and appeal workflows
- Multilingual page templates

## Phase 4 - Data and Search

- Stronger search and filtering
- Versioned source logs
- Public transparency summaries
- Import review workbench
- Geographic coverage dashboards

## Phase 5 - Private Creator Readiness

Private creator hosting remains scaffold-only until legal, consent, payment, age-gate, DMCA, privacy, storage, and moderation gates are ready.

This phase does not launch adult uploads, subscriptions, explicit content, processor flows, or private media sales from the first-wave MVP.
