# CLITZ.xyz Project Brief

CLITZ.xyz is a women-centered public platform for safety, health, privacy, memory, mutual aid, reviews, prayer, debate, and creator scaffolding.

The first public MVP layer is CLITZ Galaxy Core, a proprietary WordPress capability built for privacy-first records, moderated submissions, safe maps, directories, legal placeholder pages, and audit trails.

## Core Promise

CLITZ is built for women who need care, witness, verification, privacy, and practical support without being forced into unsafe exposure.

The project centers:

- women-centered safety records
- missing women and girls visibility
- provider and service directories
- survivor-sensitive location defaults
- mutual aid and fundraiser support
- female-review systems with moderation
- prayer and debate scaffolding
- protected creator profile reuse
- legal and safety disclaimers pending attorney review
- auditability before public claims

## Public Scope

This repository is a protected public project surface. It documents the project, public boundary, status, visuals, WordPress page draft, and launch posture.

It does not publish source code, deployment secrets, private workflows, raw reports, moderation queues, adult/private lanes, or sensitive records.

## First-Wave MVP

The first-wave MVP prioritizes:

- maps and directories
- pending front-end submissions
- moderation and verification states
- abuse and update reports
- CSV import/export workflow
- legal placeholder pages
- privacy-safe public rendering
- supervised WordPress deployment runbook

## Later Phases

Later phases may add multilingual expansion, stronger community tools, object storage, custom app migration, paid membership, and private creator monetization after legal, payment, security, and consent gates are complete.
